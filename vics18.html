The Volcanic Impacts on Climate and Society (VICS) Working Group is part of the Past Global Changes (PAGES) program within Future Earth. You can visit the main VICS webpage at PAGES by following this link. Thank you to those who joined us in Tucson in January of 2018 for our 3rd meeting, which focused on collaborative multidisciplinary preparation of the manuscripts we proposed and outlined in Zaragoza and an expanded exploration of avenues for outreach. The program for that meeting is available here [PDF]. Our 4th meeting, 'Beyond the Common Era', was held from April 13th through April 16th, 2019 in Cambridge. Our 5th meeting was originally planned for Aarhus, Denmark, but was postponed due to the pandemic. The 5th meeting will now be held in Bern, Switzerland in May 2023.
